About the Solar System

Our solar system consists of the Sun and everything that orbits around it — planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and more! The eight planets revolve around the Sun due to its strong gravitational pull. Each planet has unique characteristics, and together they make our solar neighborhood a fascinating place to explore!

From the rocky terrains of Mercury and Mars to the gas giants Jupiter and Saturn, each planet has its own story. Let’s dive in and learn more about these amazing celestial bodies!
